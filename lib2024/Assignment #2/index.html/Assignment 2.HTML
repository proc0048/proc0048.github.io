Support your local libraries! We have many fun programs, resources and much more. All resources are free. Come register for a library card. If you need support in school or need to do research, we have resources and databases you can search through to help you achieve what you want. You can research independently or you can set up a reference meeting to receive help from the library staff who are trained in finding research!

Looking less on the side of research? We have plenty of fun fictional novels or recreational materials you can choose from as well. We also have plenty of fun and engaging programs that you can try out. Learn a new skill like crocheting or knitting, or come play board games or video games. We have it all.

The library values diversity, freedom of thought, and the right to privacy. We value freedom to information for everyone, regardless of background, financial status, race or any other category. Everyone is welcome at the library. As long as you follow our policies, come relax in our spaces quietly and peacefully.

We want everyone to feel welcome at the library, which is why there is no need to pay for most of our programs or any of our materials/resources. We just want you to enjoy your time and value your stay. Come visit us if you have any questions and we will be happy to answer them for you.
