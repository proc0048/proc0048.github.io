Papercraft

A fun website with crafty ways to make stuff out of paper.

Home page

Here are some fun crafts you might want to try out:



Name tag:

You will need: Paper, scissors, clear tape, hole puncher, crayons, and yarn.

  1. Cut out a rectangle shape for your name tag
  2. Write your name on the rectangle. Make it fun to look at!
  3. Use the clear tape to turn your paper into a card. tape nice rows of tape on the front and back
  4. Use the hole puncher to punch a hole in the narrow side of the rectangle
  5. loop a peice of yarn through the hole. Tie it.
  6. Enjoy! Mabye show off your name tag to your freinds!

Personal bag:

You will need: Paper, scissors, tape (any color), Sealable bag, and crayons.

  1. Fold your peice of paper in half
  2. Tape the edges of the folded paper. Make sure the top is not taped.
  3. Do a colorful design on the frnt of the bag
  4. Cut the sealable bag in half. Use the half with the zip mouth
  5. cut the sides of the sealable bag. But don't cut the zip!!
  6. Finaly, put the cut end of the sealable bag on top of your paper bag that you made. And you have your bag!
  7. Try putting somthing into it. Mabye even tape the bottom so it won't break.

Trading cards:

You will need: crayons, scissors, markers, and card stock paper

  1. Just like you did with the name tag, cut out a rectangle card shape
  2. Think of something to trade. Some ideas are sweets, landmarks, states, boats, cars, and beach items
  3. Write one item from your chosen collection on the paper. Add some fun stats (e.i. sweetness meter: 7 points)
  4. If you did your card in pencil, you can trace it in marker and shade over it in crayon
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you have about 10 cards
  6. Show it to your friends. If it goes well, make more! Enjoy!
